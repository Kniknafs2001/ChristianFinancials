Our Services

Reconciliation Services

Our most popular service offering is simple and affordable. We will review your chart of accounts in QuickBooks to ensure all accounts are set up properly and all income and expenses are categorized correctly. We will reconcile your accounts to your bank statements, credit card statements, loans, and third-party statements, (i.e. Shopify, Stripe, or PayPal). Financial reports will be provided, giving you a snapshot of your profitability on a monthly basis.

QuickBooks Cleanup

Whether you are starting a new business or have fallen behind with your books, Christian Financial Services LLC can help. We will create a chart of accounts based on your industry type and all of your accounts will be reconciled (i.e. bank, credit card, 3rd party apps, and loans). Upon completion, a Balance Sheet and Monthly Profit and Loss Statement will be provided for your review.
